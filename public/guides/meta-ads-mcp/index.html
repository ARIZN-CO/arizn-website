Overview

What is Meta Ads MCP?

Meta launched an MCP server that lets AI agents like Claude access and control your Meta Ads account directly. You can create campaigns, set budgets, analyze performance and optimize ads just by having a conversation with Claude.

Setup

Less than 60 seconds.

  1. Open Claude at claude.ai
  2. Click Customize in the top right corner
  3. Select Connectors
  4. Click Add custom connector
  5. Paste this URL: mcp.facebook.com/ads
  6. Authorize your Meta account
  7. Done.
